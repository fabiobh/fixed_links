Privacy Policy - Illustrated Children's Book

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

Introduction

Illustrated Children's Book ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application ("App"), available on the Apple App Store.

Our App enables users to create personalized illustrated children's books using artificial intelligence. We take privacy seriously, particularly regarding content created for children.

Information Collection

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Credentials: Email address and profile information when you sign in using Apple Sign-In or other authentication methods.
  • Creative Content: Character names, story themes, and preferences you provide to generate books.
  • Generated Content: Stories and illustrations created within the App.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Data: Device model, iOS version, and unique identifiers.
  • App Usage: Features accessed, session duration, and interaction patterns.
  • Performance Data: Crash logs and diagnostics to improve App stability.

Third-Party Data

  • Apple Sign-In: Basic profile information as authorized by your Apple ID settings.
  • In-App Purchases: Transaction records processed through Apple. We do not store payment instruments.

Use of Information

We process your information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Create personalized illustrated books based on your input.
  • Account Management: Maintain your profile, preferences, and saved books.
  • Purchase Processing: Manage subscriptions and credit purchases.
  • App Enhancement: Analyze usage to improve performance and features.
  • Community Features: Enable optional sharing of books with other users.
  • Support Services: Address your questions and technical issues.
  • Security Measures: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access.

Data Storage and Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Secure Infrastructure: Data stored on encrypted cloud platforms (Cloudflare, Supabase).
  • Transport Security: All data transmission uses TLS/SSL encryption.
  • Access Management: Strict access controls limit data access to authorized personnel.
  • Retention Policy: Data retained while your account is active; deletion available upon request.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Sharing occurs only in these circumstances:

  • Service Partners: Trusted providers who help operate our App under confidentiality agreements.
  • Community Sharing: Publicly shared books are visible to other App users.
  • Legal Obligations: When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Business Transactions: In connection with corporate reorganization or asset transfer.

Third-Party Services

Our App integrates with:

  • Apple Services: Authentication, App Store distribution, and in-app purchases
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Supabase: User authentication and database services
  • Cloudflare: Content delivery and image storage
  • Google AI (Gemini): AI-powered story and image generation

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

While our App creates content designed for children, it is intended for use by parents and guardians:

  • Age Requirement: Users must be 13 years or older, or have parental consent.
  • Parental Supervision: We encourage parents to supervise children's use of the App.
  • No Direct Child Data Collection: We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.
  • COPPA Compliance: We adhere to the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act requirements.

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13 without appropriate consent, please contact us immediately for removal.

Your Privacy Rights

All Users

You may:

  • Access your personal information held by us
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw Consent for optional features like community sharing

California Residents (CCPA)

You have additional rights including:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to request deletion
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

European Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights including:

  • Right to access and data portability
  • Right to rectification and erasure
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

In-App Purchases

We offer:

  • Subscription Plans: Monthly access to book generation features
  • Credit Packs: One-time purchases for generation credits

Purchases are processed securely through Apple's App Store. Apple handles all payment information; we do not receive or store your payment details.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards protect your information during any transfer, including standard contractual clauses where applicable.

Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes become effective upon posting. We will notify you of material changes through the App or via email.
