Privacy Policy

Polygot Video Player
Last Updated: December 9, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Polygot Video Player ("we," "our," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection.

Key Points: Polygot Video Player is a free application with no advertisements and no in-app purchases. We prioritize your privacy and minimize data collection.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We DO NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't do:

  • We do not collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts
  • We do not collect browsing history or search queries
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not share any data with third parties

2.2 Information Accessed Locally

To provide video playback functionality, the App may access:

  • Media Files: Access to your video files stored on your device (only files you explicitly choose to play)
  • App Preferences: Settings and preferences stored locally on your device (such as playback settings, subtitle preferences, etc.)

This information remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

3. How We Use Information

The only data accessed by the App is used exclusively for:

  • Playing video files you select
  • Remembering your app preferences and settings
  • Providing core video player functionality

All data remains stored locally on your device and is never sent externally.

4. Data Storage and Security

All app preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using standard secure storage mechanisms provided by your operating system. We do not maintain any servers or databases that store your information.

Your media files are accessed only when you explicitly select them for playback and remain in their original location on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

Polygot Video Player does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external APIs. The App operates entirely offline and independently.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction). Since we don't collect personal information from any users, the App is safe for users of all ages.

7. Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access Control: You choose which video files the App can access
  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the App removes all local preferences and settings
  • Permission Management: You can revoke storage permissions at any time through your device settings

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. International Users

Since we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there are no international data transfer concerns. The App operates entirely on your local device regardless of your location.

10. Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable privacy laws and regulations
